Research Topics


The research program we work on in the ICT laboratory of USTH targets two main applicative axes:
– Cultural heritage preservation and promotion.
– Environmental protection and environmental risk management.
There are various problems and challenges linked to these two applicative fields, but the underlying scientific axes of research are common. The main scientific axes that we consider in this research program are: – Modelling
– Image Processing and Analysis
– Geographical Information Systems
– Machine Learning
– Expert user interaction
– Information Retrieval
– Sensors
